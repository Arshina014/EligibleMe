💡 Awareness Tips

Important things every citizen should know about government schemes

📋

Keep Documents Ready

Always keep your Aadhaar Card, Income Certificate, Caste Certificate, and Bank Passbook updated and ready. Most schemes require these basic documents.

⏰

Apply Before Deadlines

Many scholarship and welfare schemes have strict deadlines. Check the last date regularly and apply well in advance to avoid last-minute technical issues.

🏛️

Use Official Portals Only

Always apply through official government websites (.gov.in or .nic.in). Beware of fake websites and agents who charge money for free government schemes.

🔄

Check Eligibility Regularly

Government launches new schemes regularly. Check your eligibility periodically as new benefits may become available based on changes in your life situation.

📱

Use mParivahan & DigiLocker

Store your documents digitally using DigiLocker and UMANG apps. These are accepted by most government departments and make applications easier.

🤝

Visit Your CSC Center

Common Service Centres (CSCs) in your area can help you apply for various government schemes if you face difficulties applying online.
